CPDSE is launching a new open-access curriculum module on machine learning applications in drug development, available to students and educators across both partner universities.

The module spans six weeks and covers predictive modelling for ADMET properties, compound activity prediction, and interpretable AI for regulatory contexts. Teaching materials will be published on our GitHub repository.
