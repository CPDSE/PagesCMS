CPDSE brings together 60+ people across seven university departments at two universities: the University of Copenhagen and the University of Southern Denmark.

We have distinct strengths in pharmaceutical sciences, data science, and science education — and a shared belief that the pharmaceutical workforce of the future needs to speak both languages fluently.

## Our People

Our community includes:

- Students at bachelor, master, and PhD levels
- University professors and researchers
- Pharmacists and pharmaceutical industry professionals
- Data scientists and software developers
- Science educators and curriculum designers
