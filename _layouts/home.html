About CPDSE

Pharmaceutical sciences,
boosted by data science

CPDSE is dedicated to developing research-based education for the future workforce of drug experts — spanning bachelor, master, PhD and life-long learning levels. Pharmaceutical scientists will become bilinguals speaking both "data and drugs".

We are 60+ people across seven university departments at two universities, with distinct strengths in pharmaceutical sciences, data science, and science education.

Timeline

Our Journey

From our foundation to today, key milestones have shaped how CPDSE educates and connects the pharmaceutical data science community.

Center Foundation

CPDSE was established to bridge the gap between pharmaceutical sciences and data science education, creating the first structured pathway for students to become truly bilingual in both fields.

  • Initial partnerships formed
  • Curriculum framework developed
  • First cohort of students enrolled

Expansion & Innovation

Our center grew to 60+ members across seven university departments at two institutions, establishing data science facilities and launching comprehensive educational programs.

  • Data science facility established
  • Cross-institutional collaboration strengthened
  • Research initiatives launched

Strategic Growth

We continue to advance our mission by developing cutting-edge educational materials, fostering community engagement, and expanding our influence in pharmaceutical data science.

  • New workshop series launched
  • Industry partnerships developed
  • Curriculum enhancements implemented

Shaping the Future

Today, CPDSE stands as a beacon of excellence in pharmaceutical data science education, empowering students, researchers, and professionals to unlock the power of data in drug discovery and development.

  • Educating the next generation
  • Advancing research initiatives
  • Building a global community

Who we serve

Educate data & drug bilinguals

We aim to upskill and educate people that understand and can apply the right data science across the breadth of the pharmaceutical value chain.

Students

We provide consultations to help you enter data science, overcome project challenges, and build data literacy. We run workshops and coding events, creating a safe and collaborative space.

Teachers & Researchers

We develop hands-on educational materials to embed and expand data science in university curricula, while training educators and providing data-driven methodological expertise.

Life-long Learners

We reach beyond university borders by creating opportunities for networking and continuous knowledge exchange through workshops, symposiums, and community events.
