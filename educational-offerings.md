CPDSE develops and supports educational offerings across bachelor, master, PhD, and life-long learning levels.

## Convergence Talks

Convergence Talks are regular 30-minute talks covering a wide range of topics at the intersection of pharmaceutical science and data science. They are free to attend — join us in person or online. Upcoming talks are listed in the [events calendar](/PagesCMS/news-events/).

## Pharma Code Club

The Pharma Code Club is open to all students enrolled at SDU or KU. Whether you are just starting out or already comfortable with data science tools, the club is a collaborative space to practice, share, and grow together. [Find out more about the Code Club](/PagesCMS/codeclub/).

## Open Source Materials

We are committed to making pharmaceutical data science education openly accessible. All curriculum materials — datasets, notebooks, exercises, and more — are being continuously uploaded to our open repository. [Browse the materials on GitHub](https://github.com/CPDSE-EDUX).
