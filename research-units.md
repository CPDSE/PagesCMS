CPDSE brings together research units from the University of Copenhagen and the University of Southern Denmark, each contributing unique expertise to pharmaceutical data science.

## What We Work On

Our research activities focus on:

- **Data-driven methodologies** in pharmaceutical sciences
- **Educational research** — how people learn data science in a pharma context
- **Cross-disciplinary projects** combining pharmaceutical science, statistics, machine learning, and science education
